# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated: October 23, 2025**

## 1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Euno ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website at askeuno.com ("Site") or use our Service.

## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

### 2.1 Types of Cookies

**Session Cookies:**
- Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
- Essential for website functionality

**Persistent Cookies:**
- Remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them
- Remember your preferences for future visits

**First-Party Cookies:**
- Set by Euno directly
- Used for essential site functionality and preferences

**Third-Party Cookies:**
- Set by external services we use (analytics, payment processing)
- Subject to the privacy policies of those third parties

## 3. Cookies We Use

### 3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the Site to function and cannot be disabled.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|-------------|---------|----------|------|
| `connect.sid` | Session management | Session | First-party |

**Purpose**: Authentication, security, maintaining your session while you use Euno.

**Legal Basis**: Legitimate interest (necessary for service provision).

### 3.2 Functionality Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |

**Purpose**: Remember your preferences to provide a better user experience.

**Legal Basis**: Consent (can be disabled without affecting core functionality).

## 6. Cookie Consent

### 6.1 Consent Banner
When you first visit Euno, you'll see a cookie consent banner allowing you to:
- **Accept All**: Allow all cookies (essential + functional)
- **Essential Only**: Allow only necessary cookies
- **Customize**: Choose which cookie categories to enable

### 6.2 Implied Consent
By continuing to use the Site after seeing the banner, you consent to our use of strictly necessary cookies.

### 6.3 Withdrawal of Consent
You can withdraw consent at any time by:
- Clicking "Cookie Settings" in the footer
- Adjusting your browser settings
- Deleting cookies from your device

### 6.4 Consent Storage
Your cookie preferences are stored in the `cookie_consent` cookie to remember your choice.

## 7. Managing Cookies

### 7.1 Browser Controls

**Chrome:**
1. Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
2. Choose blocking preferences
3. View and delete existing cookies

**Firefox:**
1. Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
2. Manage cookie exceptions
3. Clear stored cookies

**Safari:**
1. Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
2. Block or manage cookies
3. Remove stored data

**Edge:**
1. Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data
2. Configure cookie blocking
3. Clear browsing data

### 7.2 Mobile Browsers

**iOS Safari:**
- Settings → Safari → Block All Cookies

**Android Chrome:**
- Chrome app → Settings → Site settings → Cookies
- Enable/disable as preferred

### 7.3 Cookie Preference Center

Access your cookie preferences on Euno:
1. Click "Cookie Settings" in the footer
2. Toggle cookie categories on/off
3. Save your preferences

**Location**: Footer of every page → "Cookie Settings"

## 8. Impact of Disabling Cookies

### 8.1 Essential Cookies Disabled
If you block essential cookies:
- Cannot log in to your account
- Cannot maintain active session
- Cannot process payments
- Security features may not work

**Recommendation**: Do not disable essential cookies.

### 8.2 Functional Cookies Disabled
If you block functional cookies:
- Preferences not remembered between sessions
- Must re-select data sources each time
- Theme preference resets
- Slightly reduced user experience

**Impact**: Minimal - core features still work.

### 8.3 Analytics Cookies Disabled
If you block analytics cookies (when/if implemented):
- No impact on functionality
- We cannot improve user experience based on usage data
- Your usage not tracked

**Impact**: None on your experience.

## 9. Do Not Track (DNT)

### 9.1 DNT Support
We respect "Do Not Track" browser signals.

### 9.2 DNT Behavior
If your browser sends DNT=1:
- We will not set analytics cookies (when/if implemented)
- Essential cookies still required for functionality
- Your preference logged but not tracked

### 9.3 Enabling DNT

**Most Browsers:**
- Settings → Privacy → Do Not Track
- Enable "Send Do Not Track requests"

### 10.2 No Tracking
Logged-out users are not tracked beyond what's necessary for site security.

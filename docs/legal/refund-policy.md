# Refund Policy

**Last Updated: October 23, 2025**

At Euno, we want you to be completely satisfied with our service. This Refund Policy outlines our approach to refunds and cancellations.

## 1. Overview

We offer a fair and transparent refund policy designed to protect both you and Euno. Please review this policy carefully before subscribing.

## 2. Starter (Free) Tier

The Starter tier is free and does not involve any payments. No refunds are applicable.

## 3. Paid Subscriptions (Professional & Enterprise)

### 3.1 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

**For New Subscribers:**
- Full refund available within 30 days of your first payment
- Applies to both monthly and annual subscriptions
- No questions asked

**How to Request:**
- Email support@askeuno.com with subject "Refund Request"
- Include your account email and reason (optional)
- Refunds processed within 5-7 business days

### 3.2 After 30 Days

**Monthly Subscriptions:**
- No refunds for partial months
- You may cancel anytime to prevent future charges
- Access continues until end of current billing period

**Annual Subscriptions:**
- Pro-rated refunds available for remaining months
- Calculated as: (Months Remaining / 12) × Annual Price
- Minimum retention: 3 months (no refund if less than 9 months remaining)

**Example:** 
If you paid $1,009 for Professional Annual and cancel after 4 months:
- Refund = (8 / 12) × $1,009 = $672.67

## 4. Subscription Changes

### 4.1 Upgrades (Starter → Professional → Enterprise)
- Immediate access to new tier features
- Pro-rated charge for remainder of billing period
- No refund for previous tier

### 4.2 Downgrades (Enterprise → Professional → Starter)
- Change takes effect at end of current billing period
- No partial refunds for current period
- Access to current tier features until period ends

### 4.3 Billing Cycle Changes (Monthly ↔ Annual)
- Takes effect at next renewal
- Pro-rated credits applied when switching
- Price difference reconciled at renewal

## 5. Cancellations

### 5.1 How to Cancel
**Self-Service:**
1. Log into your Euno account
2. Go to Settings → Subscription
3. Click "Cancel Subscription"
4. Confirm cancellation

**Via Email:**
- Send request to support@askeuno.com
- Include account email
- We'll confirm within 24 hours

### 5.2 Effective Date
- Cancellation takes effect at end of current billing period
- You retain access until period ends
- No partial refunds unless within 30-day window

### 5.3 Data After Cancellation
- You have 30 days to export your data
- After 30 days, all data is permanently deleted
- Download your data before canceling

## 6. Payment Failures

### 6.1 Failed Payments
If your payment fails:
- Day 1: Email notification to update payment method
- Day 3: Second reminder
- Day 7: Final notice
- Day 8: Account downgraded to Starter tier

### 6.2 Grace Period
- 7-day grace period to update payment information
- Full access maintained during grace period
- No refunds for service during grace period

### 6.3 Reactivation
- Update payment method to reactivate
- Pay outstanding balance
- Immediate restoration of service

## 7. Exceptions and Special Cases

### 7.1 Technical Issues
If Euno experiences significant downtime or service disruption:
- We may offer pro-rated credits or refunds
- Determined on case-by-case basis
- Report issues to support@askeuno.com

### 7.2 Account Violations
No refunds if account terminated for:
- Violation of Terms of Service
- Fraudulent activity
- Abuse of service
- Chargebacks (see Section 9)

### 7.3 Enterprise Contracts
Enterprise customers with custom contracts may have different refund terms as specified in their agreement.

## 8. Refund Processing

### 8.1 Timeline
- Approval: Within 2 business days
- Processing: 5-7 business days
- Bank processing: 5-10 business days (varies by bank)
- Total time: Up to 3 weeks from request to account credit

### 8.2 Refund Method
- Refunds issued to original payment method
- Same card/account used for payment
- Cannot refund to different payment method

### 8.3 Confirmation
- Email confirmation sent when refund initiated
- Include transaction reference number
- Contact us if not received within stated timeline

## 9. Chargebacks and Disputes

### 9.1 Contact Us First
Please contact support@askeuno.com before initiating a chargeback. We'll work to resolve any issues quickly.

### 9.2 Chargeback Consequences
If you file a chargeback instead of requesting a refund:
- Account immediately suspended
- All data may be deleted
- Future subscriptions may be declined
- We reserve right to pursue dispute

### 9.3 Disputed Charges
If you see an unfamiliar charge:
1. Check if it's a renewal (auto-charged)
2. Verify with other account users
3. Contact us for clarification
4. We'll investigate and respond within 48 hours

## 10. Non-Refundable Items

The following are **not eligible** for refunds:

### 10.1 Third-Party Services
- API usage costs (OpenAI, if passed through)
- External integration fees
- Third-party service charges

### 10.2 Add-On Services
- Custom development work
- Priority support upgrades (if separate fee)
- Training services

### 10.3 Data Export Fees
- If applicable in future

## 11. Fair Use Policy

### 11.1 Refund Abuse
We reserve the right to deny refunds if we detect:
- Multiple subscriptions and refund requests
- Account creation solely to obtain refund
- Data extraction then immediate refund
- Pattern of abuse across multiple accounts

### 11.2 Investigation
- We may investigate suspicious refund requests
- May require verification of account usage
- Decision final after review

## 12. Tax Refunds

### 12.1 Sales Tax
- If sales tax was charged, it will be refunded with subscription price
- Tax refund subject to jurisdiction requirements

### 12.2 VAT (EU)
- VAT refunded according to applicable EU regulations
- May require VAT registration verification

## 13. Annual Subscription Renewals

### 13.1 Auto-Renewal Notice
- Email reminder 30 days before renewal
- Option to cancel before renewal
- Renewal charged automatically

### 13.2 Post-Renewal Refunds
- 30-day money-back guarantee applies to renewals
- If you forgot to cancel and renewed, contact us within 30 days
- Full refund available if requested promptly

## 14. Communication

### 14.1 Refund Requests
All refund requests should be sent to:

**Email**: support@askeuno.com  
**Subject Line**: Refund Request  
**Include**:
- Account email address
- Subscription tier (Professional/Enterprise)
- Billing period (Monthly/Annual)
- Payment date
- Reason for refund (optional but helpful)

### 14.2 Response Time
- Initial response: Within 24 hours
- Refund decision: Within 48 hours
- Complex cases: Up to 5 business days

## 15. Promotional Offers and Discounts

### 15.1 Promotional Pricing
- If subscribed at promotional rate, refund calculated using promotional price
- Cannot claim refund for difference between regular and promotional pricing

### 15.2 Discount Codes
- Refunds reflect actual amount paid after discount
- Discount codes cannot be transferred or refunded separately

## 16. Changes to Refund Policy

### 16.1 Policy Updates
We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Changes will be:
- Posted on this page
- Effective immediately for new subscribers
- Existing subscribers: Notified via email 30 days in advance

### 16.2 Grandfathering
- Subscribers before policy change retain original refund terms for current subscription period
- New terms apply at next renewal

## 17. Legal Rights

This policy does not affect your statutory rights under consumer protection laws in your jurisdiction. If local law provides greater refund rights, those rights apply.

## 18. Questions

For questions about our Refund Policy:

**Email**: support@askeuno.com  
**Response Time**: Within 24 hours
